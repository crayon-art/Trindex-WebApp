What inspired Trindex?

Well, looking at the current online environment of Trinidad and Tobago I noticed that unlike other more developed countries many of our stores and businesses didnt have an online presence. Often times you would need to get recommendations from friends or family about local stores. So we here at Trindex aim to make finding local stores and businesses easier.

Our Goal

To help Trinbagonians and visitors to this country find the best local stores and businesses that suits their needs.

Future Plans

We plan to implement rating systems and allow users to add their own stores and businesses that they would like to recommend to other users.
